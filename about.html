About

I'm Mateo, a digital artist based in Zaragoza, Spain. I make a wide variety of digital media by incorporating realtime rendering and graphics programming techniques.

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Informatics Engineering and I'm currently studying a Master's Degree in Robotics, Graphics and Computer Vision. I'm also currently working as a reasercher in the Graphics and Imaging Lab, on the topic of audiovisual perception in Virtual Reality.

I collaborate frequently with Pure Life, a Manchester-based music label and art collective, working with a global network of artists and musicians developing a wide variety of audiovisual productions.
