About

I'm Mateo Vallejo, a freelance digital artist and engineer based in Zaragoza, Spain. I have a Bachelor's Degree in Informatics Engineering and a Master's Degree in Robotics, Graphics and Computer Vision.

My main interests are in computer graphics, creative coding, immersive art, and interactive multimedia.

My work focuses on the application of realtime rendering and graphics programming techniques for the development of artistic productions. I make a wide variety of audiovisual productions such as interactive experiences, music videos, motion graphics, visualizers for live events and video games.

I am part of Pure Life, a Manchester-based music label and art collective, working with a global network of artists and musicians developing a wide variety of audiovisual productions.
