Services and Commissions

Music Videos and Visualizers

Music videos, album visualizers, and other video features.

I produce music videos and visualizers by employing a suite of different tools, including asset creation software (Blender, Inkscape), 3D rendering engines (Unity, Godot, TiXL, custom), and video editing software (Adobe Premiere, DaVinci Resolve). When making music videos, I focus on the application of graphics programming techniques for visual effects along other creative coding techniques such as procedural generation and audio visualization.

Examples

Multiple visualizers for the Pure Live II online festival

Album Visualizer for Hyetal - Red Gates

Live Visuals
