Pure Live Festival

Pure Live was an online festival that took place on the 30th to the 31st May 2020, involving 26 artists over 2 days, and was followed up Pure Live II on 18th and 19th July 2021, which involved 34 artists over 2 days. The events raised over $2500 for the anti suicide charity CALM (Campaign Against Living Miserably).

Description

Pure Live was a series of online music festivals organized by music label Pure Life, showcasing artists from the label and community. The festivals united artists and fans in raising money for the suicide prevention organization CALM (Campaign Against Living Miserably).

For the first edition, I developed a standalone visualizer that was employed to record visuals for some of the live sets. I also made a full visual version of THUGWIDOW's set, expanding the concept of the music video I originally made for THUGWIDOW's joint release with artist VVV.

For the second edition, I made the visuals for 33 of the 34 sets (with Dystopik making the visuals for Elegance of the Damned's set), making custom visualizers adapted to each of the artists' styles while playing with the main theme of including Pure Life's logo as the main element.
